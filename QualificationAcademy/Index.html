Save Management

This game makes use of local storage when saving your game. This can leave your save vulnerable to corruption or loss. If you are concerned about your save, you can export it to your device to back it up. If you wish to restore your save, you can import it from your device.

Backup your save by exporting it to your device.

Restore your save by importing it from your device.

Restart your game by deleting your save.
